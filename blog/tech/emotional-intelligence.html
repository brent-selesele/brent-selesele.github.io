Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is all about understanding and managing our feelings and the feelings of others. It's like having a superpower that helps us communicate better, make friends, and handle tough situations. It means being able to recognize when we feel happy, sad, angry, or scared and knowing how to express those feelings in a healthy way. It also means being able to understand how other people are feeling and being kind and supportive to them when they need it. Having good emotional intelligence can make us happier, healthier, and more successful in life!

How is EQ different to IQ?

IQ, or intelligence quotient, is a measure of how smart someone is. It's usually measured by things like taking tests, solving puzzles, or doing math problems. On the other hand, EQ, or emotional intelligence, is all about understanding and managing our feelings and the feelings of others. It's not about how smart we are in a traditional sense, but about how well we can communicate, get along with others, and handle tough situations.

Why is EQ important?

Eq is important because it helps build strong relationships: When we have good EQ, we're better able to understand and communicate with other people. This helps us make friends, get along with others, and build strong relationships.It also helps with Managing our emotions: Sometimes we feel happy, sad, angry, or scared, and that's okay! But it's important to know how to handle those feelings in a healthy way. When we have good EQ, we're better able to manage our emotions and express them in a way that doesn't hurt ourselves or others.
