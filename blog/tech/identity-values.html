Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why?

I worked security and bounced at certain night clubs for a number of years. There were always issues with intoxication and being clear when dealing with certain patrons. We found that as long as communication was clear on the radio we were able to get a respond team there faster and just documenting incidents after every night we were able to see which incident occured more and we were able to put strategies in place to minimize them.
